The Composio Timeline

From a shared vision between two IIT Bombay roommates to a $29M AI infrastructure company. Explore the key milestones that shaped Composio's remarkable journey.

July 2023
Funding

The Beginning: Seed Funding & Foundation

Composio was founded by Soham Ganatra and Karan Vaidya, two former physics Olympiad teammates and IIT Bombay roommates. They secured their first seed funding round to build AI agent integration infrastructure.

The founders, having previously led engineering teams focused on integrations, witnessed firsthand the challenges of connecting complex systems and decided to solve this problem for the AI era.

Key Achievement: Initial seed funding secured

Late 2023 - Early 2024
Product Development

Building the Foundation

Intensive product development phase focusing on creating the core platform architecture. The team tackled complex challenges including multi-agent coordination, authentication across enterprise systems, and building scalable infrastructure.

Started developing the revolutionary shared learning layer for AI agents and began building integrations with popular tools and services.

Key Achievement: Core platform architecture and initial integrations completed

2024
Community Growth

Developer Community & Traction

Focused on building a strong developer community and gaining initial traction. The platform began attracting developers who needed reliable AI agent integrations.

Media coverage increased with 3 events covered by media, including company updates and product announcements.

Key Achievement: Growing developer adoption and community engagement

March 25, 2025
Series A

Series A Funding Round

Secured $24.3M in Series A funding led by Lightspeed Venture Partners and Elevation Capital. This marked a significant milestone in the company's growth trajectory.

The funding round validated the market need for AI agent integration infrastructure and positioned Composio for rapid scaling.

Key Achievement: Major institutional funding from top-tier VCs

July 22, 2025
Major Milestone

$29M Total Funding Achievement

Announced $25 million in Series A funding led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, bringing the total funding to $29 million. The round included prestigious angel investors and institutional backers.

Notable participants included Guillermo Rauch (Vercel CEO), Dharmesh Shah (HubSpot founder), Gokul Rajaram, Soham Mazumdar (Rubrik Co-founder), alongside SV Angel, Blitzscaling Ventures, and Agent Fund.

Press Coverage: Major PR announcement

August 2025
Scale Achievement

100,000+ Developers & Enterprise Adoption

Reached the significant milestone of 100,000+ developers using the platform, with 200+ companies adopting Composio for their AI agent infrastructure needs.

Major enterprise customers include Glean Technologies, and top YC startups like April, OpenNote, Airweave, Den, and Dash. The platform also gained adoption from teams at Meta, Salesforce, and Cisco.

Key Achievement: Six-figure revenue milestone and massive developer adoption

September 2025
Product Innovation

Advanced Learning Infrastructure

Launched advanced learning infrastructure that enables AI agents to improve through experience. The platform now supports 3,000+ integrations and processes millions of requests daily.

The reinforcement learning layer allows AI agents to build intuition from experience, share knowledge across the platform, and continuously improve performance - solving the fundamental limitation of static AI systems.

Key Achievement: Revolutionary AI learning infrastructure with network effects
